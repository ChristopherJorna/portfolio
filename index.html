About Me

I'm a film director and virtual production operator based in Madrid. I direct films and build the VFX workflows that make them, which means half my time is on set and the other half is spent figuring out how to make the impossible shot actually work.


I learned virtual production from scratch on international stages, and I've been pushing it somewhere new ever since. AI-assisted white-wall compositing, background generation, the kind of workflows that let a small team pull off what used to take a huge one. That work has put me on stages with Netflix and ITV, and in front of rooms at Epic Games and VP gatherings.


Right now it all points at one thing. Low Power Mode, a story about a tiny civilisation living inside a phone. I've wanted to tell it for years, and honestly every new tool I build is just me finding another way to get it made.
